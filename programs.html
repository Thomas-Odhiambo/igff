Our Program Goals

The goal of IGFF is to empower young people through our programs to:

  • Commit their lives to Jesus Christ
  • Grow in their faith and knowledge of Him
  • Share the truth with others
  • Be Christian leaders now and in the future
  • Enjoy and share God's love
  • Address societal issues affecting youth

Based on 1 Timothy 4:12 - "Don't let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith and in purity."
