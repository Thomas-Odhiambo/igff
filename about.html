Our Story

International God's Family Fellowship (IGFF) was founded in 2005 by a group of young ministers who recognized the need for interdenominational unity among Christian youth. What began as a small campus fellowship in Nairobi has grown into a nationwide movement with international reach.

Our founders were inspired by Jesus' prayer in John 17:20-23 for unity among believers. They envisioned a fellowship where young Christians could grow beyond denominational boundaries while remaining grounded in biblical truth.

"Being aware of our role as ministers of the Gospel in the words of Jesus (Matthew 28:18-20), we committed to working with the young generation who are the next generation of the church."

Our Doctrinal Foundation

IGFF is committed to the essential truths of historic Christianity as revealed in Scripture. While we welcome believers from various denominational backgrounds, we unite around these core beliefs:

We believe the Bible, consisting of the Old and New Testaments, is the inspired Word of God, without error in the original writings, and the supreme authority in all matters of faith and conduct (2 Timothy 3:16-17; 2 Peter 1:20-21).

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, equal in power and glory (Matthew 28:19; 2 Corinthians 13:14).
